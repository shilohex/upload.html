Love story

In the heart of Rome, two strangers, Maria and Carlo, bumped into each other near the Trevi Fountain. Their eyes met, and in that instant, they felt a connection. They spent the day exploring the city, sharing gelato, and laughing at each other's jokes. As the sun set, they made a wish at the fountain, promising to meet again if fate allowed it. They parted ways, their hearts filled with a sweet longing. Years later, they crossed paths again at the same fountain. This time, they decided not to leave their meeting to chance. From that day forward, Maria and Carlo were inseparable, their love story forever tied to the magic of Rome.
